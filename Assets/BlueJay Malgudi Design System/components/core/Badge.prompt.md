Small pill label for categories, statuses and priorities.

```jsx
<Badge tone="brand">Maintenance</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" dot>Operational</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" dot>Needs Attention</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" dot>Non-Functional</Badge>
<Badge tone="sand">Amenities</Badge>
```

Tones map to the semantic scale: `neutral | brand | success | warning | danger | info | sand`. Add `dot` for live-status contexts (service health, availability).
